## Project Conventions

- We will always converse in Chinese.
- The project must maintain a default `.gitignore` and keep it synchronized during development. It should proactively identify and exclude content that must not be pushed to the remote repository, including temporary intermediate files, non-final generated files, caches, logs, build artifacts, local environment files, and agent-related local configuration.
